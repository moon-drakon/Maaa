Music Controls Help

Playing the Music

The background music should start automatically when you interact with the page. If it doesn't start, you can:

  • Click anywhere on the page to enable audio
  • Click the play button in the music player at the bottom right
  • Press the Space key to toggle play/pause

Adjusting Volume

You can adjust the music volume in several ways:

  • Use the volume slider in the music player
  • Press M key to mute/unmute

Gallery Navigation

In the gallery section, you can:

  • Toggle between grid and carousel views with the buttons at the top
  • Click on any image to view it in fullscreen mode
  • Use arrow keys to navigate through images in fullscreen mode
  • Press Esc to exit fullscreen mode

Reading the Dedication

The dedication page contains special text that might be cut off in some browsers. To ensure you can read it properly:

  • All text is now left-aligned for better readability
  • The special stories about elephants and ants are highlighted
  • If text still appears cut off, try zooming out slightly with Ctrl + -

Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues:

  • Make sure your browser is updated to the latest version
  • Try refreshing the page if elements don't load properly
  • Check that your device's sound is on and not muted
  • For mobile devices, ensure media autoplay is enabled in your browser settings
